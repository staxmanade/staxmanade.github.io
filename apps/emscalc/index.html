EMS Calc

EMT Calc keeps the most used scales and calculations in EMS at hand and easy to use.

  • Trauma
    • Revised Trauma Score
  • Medical
    • Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS)
    • Ramsey Sedation Scale
  • Pediatric
    • Apgar Score
    • Pediatric Glasgow Coma Scale (PGCS)
    • Pediatric Dosing

Created not only as a learning tool but also as a quick reference to commonly forgotten scales and calculations for field EMS personnel. It offers dozens of commonly used pediatric dosing for easy accessibility.

EMT Calc is a must have for anyone in EMS or EMT school or a professional on the go.
