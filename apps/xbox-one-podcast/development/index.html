Xbox One Podcast Player - See what it's all about...

Who wants to see an Xbox One Podcast Player?

I Want One!

I am in the midst of building a desperately missing app for Xbox One. A Podcast Listener (Podcatcher)

If you, like myself, felt like the Xbox One platform was missing an essential Podcast player app, then you have come to the right place.

Imagine how great it would be to listen to your favorite podcasts while you are also playing Xbox One games or apps, or in the background while you work out in your game room, do dishes in the kitchen or whatever else you do while you listen to your favorite podcasts.

I wanted this app to exist as well - so I am embarking on the journey to build it. Now, if you would like to follow along during this journey, or participate by filling out surveys (they'll help determine what features make it in to the initial version), offering opinions or critiques during the development phase, then I would encourage you to sign up below and gain exclusive access to me (the developer) during the entire process.

Who am I?

Hi, my name is Jason. I am a father, husband, software developer and a huge fan of podcasts.

I recently acquired an Xbox One, where I found out quickly that I could not play my favorite podcasts while I played Minecraft with my daughter. So, through some connections I have and my previous experience building Xbox One apps, I decided to jump on the opportunity to develop the application that I wanted to have.

How can you participate?

Since I am building this app as an independent developer, meaning I am doing this with my own time/money with what little free-time I have between a 'normal job' and raising a family, I would love the opportunity to share this development process with you as I go.

If you sign up, you will be given the opportunity to provide valuable feedback in the feature development, prioritization and overall functionality of the Xbox One Podcast player.

Let's get this app build so we can start enjoying Podcasts on Xbox One.
