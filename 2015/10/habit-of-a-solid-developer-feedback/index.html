This follows a very similar vein to failing fast, but don't just try to catch your self from failing, but outwardly try to look for ways to improve.

Ask others for feedback. Don't wait for your companies 1/2 year or once a year review process to get feedback on how you're doing.

Possibly offer others feedback (but careful how you word it and be sure you have the trust and the ear of the person).

Tools to fail fast

  • Design (actually think about it...)
  • Compiler (Best first automated test)
  • Unit tests and integration tests
  • QA

Learn the tried and true programming patterns and practices

  • throw exceptions
  • design by contract
  • principle of least surprise
  • S.O.L.I.D.
  • Gang of Four

Happy Improved Feedback Cycle!
